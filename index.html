THE TECHNO-FORUM

Great Thoughts reduced to practice become Great Acts.

This is an organization of CSE students of the college. The forum aims at improving the technical and industrial knowledge of the students and strives to keep them abreast with latest softwares and technologies evolving in the information technology field. It welcomes the freshers into the world of computer engineering by conducting essential orientation classes.
It organizes talks by eminent personalities in the industry on evolving technologies in computing, workshops on latest developing platforms, languages and software packages in the IT industry and helps coordinate and implement software projects for the students in the Computer Science & Engineering Dept.
